We’ve launched a blog.

It’s something we’ve been meaning to do for a while — a simple place to share updates, stories, and useful information from The Anchor Inn.

## What you’ll find here

- Updates on events and live music  
- News about the pub, kitchen, and rooms  
- Local recommendations and guides  
- Occasional behind-the-scenes bits  

## Why we’ve added this

The website already covers the essentials — food, drinks, rooms, and what’s on.

The blog gives us a bit more flexibility. It’s where we can share things that don’t quite fit anywhere else, or go into a bit more detail when it’s useful.

## Keep an eye out

We’ll keep this updated as things happen — whether that’s a busy weekend, a new menu, or something interesting going on locally.

If there’s something you’d like to see here, feel free to let us know.
